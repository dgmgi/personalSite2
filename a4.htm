A4: Invitation Design
1. How can we improve this?

- I think the details are a bit too much, especially on the wording. We can remove some of the details to make it more “to the point”. We can also make an invitation card rather than just sending this text to a person to have a more “personal” feel to it, like their presence is of most importance.


2. What would be the objective function?

- The passage is an invitation to Robert and Alexandra’s after-dinner party. The invitation tells you what time, date, and where it will occur. The invitation also has an RSVP, requiring the one given the invitation to tell the party owners of they can attend or not.
